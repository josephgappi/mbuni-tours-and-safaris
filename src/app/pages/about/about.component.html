Our Story

Founded in 2020, Mbuni Tours & Safaris was born from a deep passion for Africa's breathtaking landscapes and incredible wildlife. Our name "Mbuni" means ostrich in Swahili, symbolizing our commitment to providing unique perspectives on African safaris.

With over a decade of combined experience in the tourism industry, our team of expert guides and travel consultants is dedicated to crafting personalized safari experiences that go beyond the ordinary.

We believe in sustainable tourism that benefits local communities while preserving Africa's natural heritage for future generations.
